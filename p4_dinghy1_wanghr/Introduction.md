### Introduction

Our game's name is ***Escape!***. Your goal is to escape the obstacles, run as far as possible and get higher scores. There are two modes: easy and hard. In hard mode you move faster and meet more obstacles. There are also flowers along the road. Your can pick them up to get a bonus.
